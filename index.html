Te-Yen Wu

I am a second year Ph.D. student at XDicovery Lab, Dartmouth College. My advisor is Professor Xing-Dong Yang. My research is toward developing novel sensing techniques and new interactive prototyping system. My goal is to enable more researchers and amateurs to explore the frontier in HCI technology. I have maintained a curated list for sensing techniques in Github.
